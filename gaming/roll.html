Vampire (v5) roll probability generator

The problem: The way VtM v5 does dice rolls makes figuring out probabilities a bit more difficult than it looks at first blush. Rather than try to math it up, I decided to do something I'm more comfortable with and stats it up.

Instructions: Plug in the number of dice being rolled, the difficulty, and how many of your dice are hunger dice. The dice roller will run a number of sims so you can get an idea of how likely you are succeed, fail, or eat your coterie.
